For your project include this
XML snippet in your pom.xml.

```
<dependency>                                                                                                                                                                                                                                
  <groupId>com.googlecode.charts4j</groupId>                                                                                                                                                                                                
  <artifactId>charts4j</artifactId>                                                                                                                                                                                                         
  <version>1.3</version>                                                                                                                                                                                                           
</dependency>                                                                                                                                                                                                                               
```

For Leiningen Clojure users please use:
> `[com.googlecode.charts4j/charts4j "1.3"]`
